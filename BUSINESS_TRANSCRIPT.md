## Business Information (Use as the Primary Source of Content)

The business is Dose by Design, an aesthetic medicine clinic focused on personalized, natural-looking cosmetic treatments.

### Founder

The founder is Cathy Tang, a board-certified Physician Assistant.

Background:

- Graduated from Massachusetts College of Pharmacy and Health Sciences in 2016.
- Approximately 10 years of experience as a Physician Assistant.
- Two years practicing Urgent Care before specializing in aesthetics.
- Over 8 years of experience in aesthetic medicine.
- Originally from Boston and relocated to California in 2018.
- Frequently attends advanced aesthetic trainings and certifications.
- Reiki certified, which she incorporates into creating a calming and comfortable patient experience, but Reiki is NOT offered as a standalone medical service.

### Why Dose by Design Exists

Dose by Design was created after Cathy left a previous clinic where she felt patient care and professional values were no longer aligned.

Rather than creating another high-volume cosmetic clinic, she wanted to build a business focused on:

- Personalized care
- Long-term patient relationships
- Education
- Natural-looking enhancements
- Honest recommendations
- Collaborative treatment planning
- High-quality patient experience

The business exists to help clients look refreshed, confident, and well-rested while still looking like themselves.

### Target Audience

Primary audience:

- Adults in their 30s and older
- Clients willing to invest in long-term aesthetic care
- Women are the primary demographic, although men are also welcome.

### Common Patient Concerns

Patients commonly seek treatment for:

- Fine lines
- Wrinkles
- Facial volume loss
- Skin laxity
- Facial asymmetry
- Looking tired
- Aging gracefully
- Preventative aesthetic treatments

### Patient Philosophy

One of the biggest differentiators is the consultation process.

Rather than pushing treatments, Cathy:

- Educates patients
- Builds personalized treatment plans
- Allows patients to move at their own pace
- Focuses on long-term relationships instead of one-time sales
- Takes approximately one hour with most patients so they never feel rushed

The tone of the website should emphasize:

- Collaboration
- Trust
- Education
- Natural beauty
- Personalized care
- Concierge-level experience

Avoid sounding salesy or overly promotional.

### Treatment Philosophy

The website should repeatedly communicate:

"Our goal isn't to change how you look. Our goal is to help you look refreshed, rested, confident, and naturally beautiful."

Avoid language suggesting dramatic transformations.

### Consultation Process

Typical patient journey:

1. Consultation
2. Discuss goals and concerns
3. Facial assessment
4. Education on treatment options
5. Personalized recommendations
6. Collaborative treatment plan
7. Treatment (if desired)
8. Long-term follow-up

Patients should feel informed, not pressured.
